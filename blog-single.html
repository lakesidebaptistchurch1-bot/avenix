This Week's Sermon Embracing Forgiveness

Feb 8, 2026 Pastor Samuel 8 min read

Embracing Forgiveness: Healing the Heart

Grace empowers us to release the past and step into freedom. This week’s message brings biblical clarity, practical steps, and a call to a restored life.

In-person & online Family friendly Live worship
Watch the Full Sermon Plan Your Visit

Why This Message Matters

Forgiveness is not a feeling, it is a courageous decision. When we surrender our pain to Jesus, we begin to experience freedom, restoration, and renewed relationships.

What to Expect

Warm welcome, uplifting worship, and a practical message for everyday life.

New Here?

We will guide you from the parking lot to a seat. Come as you are.

Need Prayer?

Our prayer team is ready to stand with you after service.

Heal the Wound

Bring your hurt to God and invite His healing presence.

Release the Debt

Let go of bitterness and open your heart to peace.

Grow in Grace

Choose mercy, wisdom, and healthy boundaries.

Forgiveness does not ignore the pain; it transforms it. Grace opens the door to freedom, restoration, and a future shaped by love.

Scripture Focus

Ephesians 4:32

“Be kind and compassionate to one another, forgiving each other, just as in Christ God forgave you.”

Three Ways to Start This Week

  1. Pray daily for the person or situation that hurt you.
  2. Write a short letter of release, even if you never send it.
  3. Take one step toward peace: a call, a conversation, or counsel.

Testimony Highlight
